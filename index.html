What We Stand For

Three pillars define the character and mission of every North Dallas Alpha — guiding our service to community, our investment in the next generation, and our bonds with one another.

Scholarship

Through the Xi Tau Lambda Foundation, we award scholarships and fund mentoring programs that open doors for the next generation of scholars and leaders across North Dallas County.

A Message From Our President

Welcome to the House of Alpha

"GOODWILL is the monarch of this house. Men, unacquainted, enter, shake hands, exchange greetings, and depart friends. Cordiality exists among all who abide within."

Welcome to Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. — Xi Tau Lambda Chapter, seated in North Dallas County. I am Brother Cedric Washington, and I have the honor of serving as the 20th Chapter President of this great chapter.

Xi Tau Lambda was chartered March 3, 1984 as the 607th seat in the House of Alpha. For over four decades, our brothers have led with character, served with purpose, and invested in the communities we call home.

Thank you for visiting our page and learning more about the North Dallas Alphas!

Bro. Cedric Washington 20th Chapter President, Xi Tau Lambda
