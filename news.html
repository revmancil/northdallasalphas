Xi Tau Lambda Prepares to Celebrate 40 Years of Service

We have provided scholarships to young men entering college as well as resources for responsible choices through Project Alpha. In our community, we have brought valuable partnerships to our city through our commitment to arts education for youth, civic participation, and mentoring of young men who are seeking guidance and direction.

From the Sankofa Project to the Alpha Legacy Scholarship Gala, four decades of Xi Tau Lambda represent a legacy of purpose-driven leadership that continues to shape North Dallas County.

MISD Partnership

A Legacy of Commitment

Since 2019, Xi Tau Lambda Chapter has been an avid supporter of MISD schools, students, and families — growing in complexity and reach each year.

2019

Scott Johnson Middle School

Xi Tau Lambda began mentoring the young men of Scott Johnson's "Boys II Men" program. Monthly, brothers presented the fraternity's Alpha Legacy curriculum to these young men, establishing a foundation of excellence and purpose.

2021

Boyd High School Expansion

Due to the resounding success and impact of its Alpha Legacy program at SJMS, McKinney ISD invited Xi Tau Lambda to expand its partnership by bringing its Alpha Legacy curriculum and monthly mentoring program to the campus of Boyd High School.

2022

John R. Roach Detention Center

McKinney ISD invited the Xi Tau Lambda Chapter to deepen its commitment to MISD by extending its mentoring program to the young men of Collin County's John R. Roach Detention Center — reaching those who need guidance most.
