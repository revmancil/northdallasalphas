Membership

An Alpha Phi Alpha Member's Greatest Interest is Outside of Himself.

What We Value

Who We're Looking For

We value those who believe that their membership in Alpha Phi Alpha is the presentation of their personal commitment to these time-valued ideals.

Knowledge

A commitment to academic excellence and lifelong learning that elevates yourself and your community.

Achievement

Striving for excellence in your chosen vocation and delivering results that reflect your character.

Service

A genuine desire to serve the communities in which you live, not just participate in them.

Integrity

Honest, principled behavior that protects the reputation and legacy of Alpha Phi Alpha.

Excellence

A standard of quality in all things — from personal conduct to professional achievement.

Community

Active, meaningful engagement with the communities we serve across North Dallas County.

"We are not looking for candidates who are not interested in serving the communities in which they live. This ship does not need anchors."

Eligibility

Membership Requirements

Any male holder of an earned baccalaureate degree from an accredited college or university may be presented as a candidate for membership in an alumni chapter provided that he meets the following:

01

Earned Baccalaureate Degree

Has been minimally conferred a 4-year baccalaureate degree from an accredited institution (includes accredited online university).

02

Good Character

Demonstrates a track record of honesty, integrity, and principled behavior in personal and professional life.

03

Registered to Vote

Is registered to vote, if eligible and qualified — a reflection of your commitment to civic engagement.

04

Member Sponsor

Is sponsored by a member in good standing from Xi Tau Lambda Chapter.

05

Member Recommendation

Is recommended by a member in good standing from Xi Tau Lambda Chapter.

06

National Research

All potential candidates are encouraged to visit www.apa1906.net to learn more about Alpha Phi Alpha.
